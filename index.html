About Templeton Fiber Optics

Templeton Fiber Optics LLC is a small and quickly growing business specializing in high-precision fiber-optic splicing, testing, and troubleshooting. With over a decade of field experience, we combine meticulous craftsmanship with state-of-the-art equipment to deliver reliable fiber infrastructure for ISPs, municipalities, and enterprise clients across the Eastern United States.

Safety and quality are at the core of our operations — we adhere to TIA/EIA standards, maintain OSHA compliance, and carry full liability insurance for peace of mind on every project.
